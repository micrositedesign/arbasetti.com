Give a Sock

As my very first attempt at designing professionally, Give a Sock was my ideal client. By keeping the development costs very low, and the client giving me enough time to complete the job, I came up with something she was very happy with. Using WordPress, and my signature custom framework, I was able to make an impact in our local community by creating a digital home for this non-profit agency. The website has been online for several years now, with another year already locked in.
